Legacy Crash

Crash was made mostly to serve the people, who party late into the night away from home. These people can not always reliably get home or they may just need a quick place to sleep. Crash was made to circumvent this, by offering a solution of very temporary places of residence. A simple allegory, crash is to the hotel business, what uber was to taxis.

The red colour that permeate the app was meant to carry energy through the pages. However it activated the danger sense more than often than not and was a mess to navigate in.
