Sweetbot and Hotbot was a group project, it was as much to show off working in groups as it was developing a website.

We made use of github as our online workspace. Through the use of branches and forks, we were able to each work on a part of the website seperately. The site had to follow a certain ruleset, certain fonts, colours and logos had to be used. Through an error on my part, the font ended up being only local, so it did not show, unless you had installed the font prior.
