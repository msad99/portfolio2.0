Updated report

Sweetbot and hotbot was the main project in the fourth flow of the first semester, this is my updated take on it.

Sweetbot had a couple of mistakes, which i worked on fixing. The first and most important error i originally made was not importing a fontface for the lato font. When this was fixed i worked on fixing the active class placement on one of the pages.
I decided to keep the spacing on the navigation bar, since i like the look of it in this case. Next I set to work on the pack solutions pricings, which were out of alignment and not centered. Fixed this by having the containing element be a relative position and then having the text have an absolute position and sending it to the bottom.
